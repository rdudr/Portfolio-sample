# Product Overview

This is a personal portfolio website for Rishabh Dangi, an Electrical Engineer specializing in IoT security, embedded systems, and product development.

## Purpose

Showcase professional background, education, experience, technical activities, projects, awards, and skills through an immersive, visually elegant single-page application.

## Design Philosophy

- Premium dark-overlay aesthetic with full-screen hero sections
- Smooth scroll-snap navigation between eight content sections
- Image carousels with floating card styling and warm bevel shadows
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Focus on visual elegance and smooth animations

## Target Audience

Potential employers, collaborators, and professional contacts seeking to understand Rishabh's qualifications, experience, and technical capabilities.
